### Description
PrivExpensIA revolutionizes expense tracking with cutting-edge AI technology while keeping your data 100% private. Using advanced on-device processing, the app instantly extracts information from receipts without any data leaving your phone.

**Key Features:**
• Smart Receipt Scanning - Advanced OCR technology reads receipts in 8 languages
• AI-Powered Extraction - Automatically detects merchant, amount, tax, and categories
• 100% Privacy - All processing on-device, no internet required
• Beautiful Glass UI - Modern frosted glass interface with smooth animations
• Instant Statistics - Real-time charts and spending insights
• Export Options - Generate PDF reports or CSV spreadsheets

**Privacy First:**
Unlike other expense apps, PrivExpensIA never sends your data to servers. No account required, no tracking, no ads. Your financial data stays on your device.

**Perfect For:**
• Privacy-conscious individuals
• Small business owners
• Freelancers and contractors
• Students managing budgets
• Anyone tracking expenses

**Technical Excellence:**
• Vision Framework for OCR
• MLX Framework with Qwen2.5 AI model
• Core Data for secure storage
• SwiftUI with glass morphism design
• 60 FPS animations
• < 2 second processing time

Experience the future of private expense tracking. Download PrivExpensIA today!
