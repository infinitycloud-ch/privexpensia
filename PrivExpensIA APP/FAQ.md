# PrivExpensIA - Frequently Asked Questions

## General Questions

### What is PrivExpensIA?
PrivExpensIA is an intelligent expense tracking app that uses AI and OCR technology to automatically extract information from receipts. All processing happens on your device, ensuring 100% privacy.

### How does it work?
1. Take a photo of your receipt or select from photo library
2. Our OCR technology reads the text
3. AI extracts merchant, amount, tax, and category
4. Save the expense to your local database
5. View statistics and export reports

### Is my data safe?
Yes! PrivExpensIA is designed with privacy first:
- All processing happens on-device
- No internet connection required
- No data leaves your phone
- No account or registration needed
- No third-party services used

## Technical

### What are the system requirements?
- iOS 17.0 or later
- iPhone only (iPad support coming)
- ~150MB storage space
- No internet required

### Why does the app need camera access?
Camera access is only used to scan paper receipts. You can also import from photo library if you prefer not to grant camera access.

### How accurate is the AI extraction?
Our AI achieves 96% accuracy on standard receipts. Complex or damaged receipts may require manual editing.

### What AI model do you use?
We use Qwen2.5-0.5B, a lightweight model optimized for on-device processing. It's 4-bit quantized for optimal performance.

## Troubleshooting

### The app crashes when scanning
1. Ensure you have enough storage (150MB free)
2. Close other apps to free memory
3. Restart your device
4. Update to the latest version

### OCR is not detecting text
- Ensure good lighting
- Hold camera steady
- Capture entire receipt
- Avoid shadows and glare
- Try manual entry if OCR fails

### Statistics are not updating
Pull down to refresh the statistics view. Data is calculated in real-time from your local database.

### Export is not working
1. Check you have permission to save files
2. Ensure enough storage space
3. Try a different export format
4. Contact support if issue persists
