## PrivExpensIA Launches Revolutionary Privacy-First Expense Tracking App for iPhone

**Paris, France - September 20, 2025**

[Author] Dang today announced the launch of PrivExpensIA, a groundbreaking expense tracking application that combines cutting-edge AI technology with uncompromising privacy protection. Available now on the App Store, PrivExpensIA processes all data directly on users' iPhones, ensuring financial information never leaves their device.

### Revolutionary Privacy Approach

In an era of increasing data breaches and privacy concerns, PrivExpensIA takes a radical approach: absolutely no data collection. The app requires no account creation, no internet connection, and processes everything using on-device AI models.

"Privacy shouldn't be a luxury feature," said [Author] Dang, creator of PrivExpensIA. "We've proven that powerful AI capabilities and absolute privacy can coexist. Your receipts, expenses, and financial data belong to you alone."

### Advanced AI Technology

PrivExpensIA leverages Apple's Vision Framework combined with the Qwen2.5 AI model, optimized to run entirely on-device. The app can:

- Extract information from receipts in under 2 seconds
- Support 8 languages automatically
- Achieve 96% accuracy in data extraction
- Categorize expenses intelligently
- Generate insights without cloud processing

### Beautiful Glass Interface

The app features a stunning "liquid glass" design with smooth animations running at 60 FPS. Every interaction is enhanced with haptic feedback, creating a premium user experience that rivals apps from major tech companies.

### Key Features

- **Smart OCR**: Instantly scan receipts with your camera
- **AI Extraction**: Automatic detection of merchant, amount, tax, and items
- **Privacy First**: 100% on-device processing, no data collection
- **Beautiful Charts**: Real-time statistics and spending insights
- **Export Options**: Generate PDF reports or CSV files
- **No Account Required**: Start using immediately, no sign-up needed

### Availability

PrivExpensIA is available now as a free download on the App Store for iPhone running iOS 17.0 or later. The app requires approximately 150MB of storage and works completely offline.

### Beta Success

During beta testing with 15 testers over two weeks, PrivExpensIA achieved:
- Zero crashes in over 1,000 scanning operations
- Average user rating of 4.8/5
- 100% of testers confirmed no privacy concerns
- Processing time consistently under 2 seconds
