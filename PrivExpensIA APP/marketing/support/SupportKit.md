# PrivExpensIA Support Kit

## FAQ - 20 Common Questions

### **General App Questions**

**Q1: What makes PrivExpensIA different from other expense trackers?**
A: PrivExpensIA is the only expense tracker that keeps 100% of your data on your device. No cloud syncing, no server uploads, no external access. Your financial privacy is completely protected while you get powerful expense tracking features.

**Q2: Is PrivExpensIA really completely offline?**
A: Yes! PrivExpensIA processes everything locally on your iPhone using Apple's Core ML framework for AI features. Your expense data never leaves your device - not even anonymized or encrypted versions.

**Q3: How much does PrivExpensIA cost?**
A: PrivExpensIA is $4.99 as a one-time purchase. No subscriptions, no in-app purchases, no hidden fees. Buy once, own forever with all future updates included.

**Q4: Will my data sync across devices?**
A: No, by design. Since we prioritize privacy, there's no cloud syncing. Your data stays exclusively on the device where you enter it. You can export your data and manually import it to another device if needed.

**Q5: Can I export my expense data?**
A: Absolutely! You can export your data anytime in CSV or JSON format. Your data belongs to you, and we make it easy to take it with you if you ever want to switch apps.

### **Features & Functionality**

**Q6: How accurate is the AI categorization?**
A: Our on-device AI categorization is highly accurate and learns from your patterns. It uses Apple's Core ML to process expense descriptions and amounts locally, improving over time without sending data anywhere.

**Q7: Can I create custom expense categories?**
A: Yes! You can create, edit, and delete custom categories to match your specific needs. The AI will learn your custom categories and suggest them for future expenses.

**Q8: Does PrivExpensIA support multiple currencies?**
A: Currently, PrivExpensIA supports the currency set in your iPhone's region settings. Multi-currency support is planned for a future update.

**Q9: Can I set budgets and spending limits?**
A: Yes! You can set monthly budgets for each category and receive alerts when you're approaching or exceeding your limits - all processed locally on your device.

**Q10: How do I add recurring expenses?**
A: In the expense entry screen, tap "Recurring" and set your frequency (daily, weekly, monthly, etc.). PrivExpensIA will automatically add these expenses to your records.

### **Technical & Privacy**

**Q11: How do you ensure my data is private?**
A: Your data never leaves your iPhone. We don't have servers to store your information, no analytics tracking, no crash reporting with personal data. Even Apple doesn't see your expense data through iCloud.

**Q12: What happens if I lose my phone?**
A: Since data is stored locally, you would lose your expense history unless you've made regular exports. We recommend periodically exporting your data as backup. Future versions will include encrypted local backup options.

**Q13: Do you track any usage data?**
A: No. We don't implement any analytics, crash reporting, or usage tracking that could compromise your privacy. We only collect App Store purchase information, which is handled entirely by Apple.

**Q14: Is my data encrypted on my device?**
A: Yes! All your expense data is encrypted using iOS's built-in encryption. Your data benefits from the same security that protects your photos, messages, and other personal information.

**Q15: What iOS version do I need?**
A: PrivExpensIA requires iOS 15.0 or later to take advantage of the latest privacy and Core ML features.

### **Troubleshooting**

**Q16: The app crashed - will you see my data?**
A: No. Since we don't implement crash reporting services, we have no way to access your data even if the app crashes. If you experience crashes, please email us with details, and we'll help troubleshoot.

**Q17: Why can't I connect my bank account?**
A: By design, PrivExpensIA doesn't connect to banks or financial institutions. This ensures your financial accounts remain completely private. You add expenses manually, which takes seconds with our streamlined interface.

**Q18: Can I import data from other expense apps?**
A: Currently, you need to manually enter your data. We're working on import tools for common export formats while maintaining our privacy standards.

**Q19: The AI categorization seems wrong - how do I improve it?**
A: Simply correct the category when you notice an error. The on-device AI learns from your corrections and becomes more accurate over time. Your corrections never leave your device.

**Q20: How do I get help if I have issues?**
A: Email us at support@privexpensia.app. While we can't see your data, we can help with app functionality, feature questions, and troubleshooting.

## Escalation Procedures

### **Level 1: Standard Support**
- **Response Time:** Within 24 hours
- **Handled By:** Support team member
- **Issues:** General questions, basic troubleshooting, feature explanations

### **Level 2: Technical Support**
- **Response Time:** Within 12 hours
- **Handled By:** Technical support specialist
- **Issues:** Complex technical problems, app crashes, data corruption, export issues

### **Level 3: Development Team**
- **Response Time:** Within 8 hours
- **Handled By:** Developer or CTO
- **Issues:** Critical bugs, privacy concerns, security issues, data loss

### **Level 4: Executive**
- **Response Time:** Within 4 hours
- **Handled By:** Founder/CEO
- **Issues:** Privacy breaches, legal concerns, media escalations, major business issues

### **Escalation Triggers:**
- Customer mentions "privacy breach" or "data leak"
- Legal threat or compliance concern
- Media inquiry or public criticism
- Request for executive contact
- Repeated unresolved technical issues
- App Store rating threat below 3 stars

### **Escalation Process:**
1. Support team identifies escalation need
2. Brief context summary prepared
3. Escalation email sent to next level
4. Original support member remains involved
5. Customer informed of escalation within 2 hours
6. Resolution timeline communicated
